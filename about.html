About Us

Built for people figuring things out.

A calmer way to move through uncertainty, one realistic step at a time.

Steady Steps is built for people figuring things out—whether you’re graduating into a tough job market or navigating a layoff.

The job search can feel overwhelming and isolating, and most tools focus on doing more, faster. This one is different. It’s designed to help you slow things down, stay consistent, and take small, realistic steps each week.

There are no promises of quick results—just a simple system to help you keep moving forward.
